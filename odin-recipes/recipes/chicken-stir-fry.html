Chicken Stir Fry

Description:

Chicken stir fry is a dish that is prepared by stir-frying chicken with vegetables in a wok. The dish is simple to make and can be prepared in a short amount of time. It is a popular dish in many Asian countries and is often served with rice or noodles.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken breast, sliced
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, etc.)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. In a small bowl, mix together soy sauce, oyster sauce, cornstarch, and chicken broth. Set aside.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a wok over high heat. Add garlic and stir-fry for 30 seconds.
  3. Add chicken and stir-fry until cooked through.
  4. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Pour in the sauce mixture and stir-fry until the sauce thickens.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot with rice or noodles.
